The Situation at a Glance

This section presents an overview of user satisfaction. It highlights the overall score and key insights from the survey, providing a solid foundation before diving into the details.

✔ A Solid and Appreciated Base

With 80% of users satisfied or very satisfied, WEbpos effectively meets fundamental needs. Ease of use and robust reporting features are major acclaimed assets.

⚠ Critical Friction Points

However, significant operational challenges emerge. System slowness, lack of integrations, and excessively long support delays are major impediments to the efficiency and complete satisfaction of users.

What Our Users Like

Here, we explore the strengths of WEbpos. Analyzing the highest-rated aspects helps us understand what constitutes our competitive advantage and what we need to preserve and strengthen in the future.

"WEbpos is very intuitive and easy to get started with."

Features: 80%

"Sales reports are very detailed and useful."

Value for Money: 80%

Perceived as a cost-effective solution by a majority of users.

Priority Areas for Improvement

This section focuses on the most significant challenges identified by our users. Understanding these friction points is essential for prioritizing our development efforts and improving the overall experience.

Ranking of Cited Problems

Detailed Problem Analysis

The Critical Bottleneck

Slowness is the most urgent problem, directly impacting client operations during peak activity periods.

  • 45% recommend improving performance.
  • 35% cite transaction speed as a major concern.
"The software is often slow, especially during peak hours, which slows down service."
The Efficiency Brake

The lack of seamless integrations forces users into manual data entry, leading to errors and wasted time.

  • 30% request new integrations.
  • 25% highlight difficulties connecting to other software.
"We had difficulties connecting WEbpos with our existing stock management system."
The Lack of Responsiveness

The issue is not the quality of the solution provided, but the waiting time to obtain it, which generates frustration and operational risk.

  • 25% recommend strengthening support.
  • 10% direct dissatisfaction regarding support.
"Support response time is sometimes too long."

Strategic Recommendations

Based on this analysis, here are the three priority action areas for our roadmap. Each recommendation aims to transform an identified weakness into a strength, thereby improving the overall value of WEbpos.

⚡

#1. Optimize Performance

  • Conduct a comprehensive performance audit (code, DB, infra).
  • Focus efforts on managing peak activity.
  • Communicate transparently on ongoing improvements.
🤝

#2. Extend Integrations

  • Identify the most requested integrations (stock, accounting, CRM).
  • Develop robust APIs and "plug-and-play" connectors.
  • Provide clear documentation and dedicated support.
📣

#3. Strengthen Support

  • Improve response times via better SLAs.
  • Develop support options (chat, knowledge base).
  • Increase resources during critical hours.
